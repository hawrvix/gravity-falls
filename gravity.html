- Gravity Falls -

The twelve-year-old brother and sister of Dipper and Mabel expected that the summer would be boring. Their parents floated them to the backwater town of Gravity Falls to the greedy and rude uncle Stan. Soon the twins discovered that Gravity Falls is actually full of real miracles. It's literally a gold mine for supernatural hunters. You can't take a step here, so as not to bump into a ghost, a mermaid, an animated statue or a time traveler. And even if you are ready for it, Gravity Falls will be able to surprise you. Already in the first episode, Mabel's mysterious boyfriend turns out not to be a vampire, as she thought, but ... a completely different fantastic creature. And not even one.
